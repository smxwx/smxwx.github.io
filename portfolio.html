Expository Paragraph: Colombian Fashion

Looking at your closet and seeing the same style of clothing every day can be tedious. Free yourself by knowing the fashion of another country! Colombian fashion is one of the best and diverse options you have. First, the variety of styles among each region of the country is huge. The characteristic clothes that you can find in the Pacific region, such as colorful dresses accompanied by floral patterns don’t look at all like some of the dark colored clothes of Boyacá. Second, the amount of clothes you can buy is quite wide, in many places in Colombia there are locations focused on selling clothes of all styles in different stores. Some examples of this are places like San Victorino or Veinte de Julio in Bogota, but in general it’s possible to get all types of clothes in most of the country. There are many options to choose from and it is possible to find something for all tastes: formal wear, sportswear, and even the most traditional clothing, but there are also all kinds of iconic accessories to complement your clothing style: necklaces, hats, earrings or even hair styles, with symbols of Colombia as nature or the colors of the flag. Colombian fashion is very diverse and if you are bored of the clothes in your closet, it can be a great option to look at!
